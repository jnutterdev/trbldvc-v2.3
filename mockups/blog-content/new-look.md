The redesign of this site was a detailed process with many versions before reaching its current look. Each version aimed to introduce something new and innovative, with earlier designs featuring blog posts on various personal topics. These included insights into programming, music production, and other subjects of interest at the time. However, none of the previous versions truly captured the original vision for this site. This is why no older content is available now. Moving forward, the site will focus on exploring and discussing selected topics like music, movies, manga, and games, especially in the areas of cyberpunk and science fiction. This shift will allow for a deeper exploration of these genres, creating content that connects more closely with the intended audience and fulfills the site's purpose.
